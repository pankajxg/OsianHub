Shipping Policy

At OSIAN, we deliver certificates, cash prizes, and merchandise to winners of our quiz competitions. This policy outlines our delivery methods and timelines for physical items.

1. Digital Delivery (Certificates)

Digital certificates are our primary delivery method. Most certificates are delivered electronically for faster access and eco-friendly distribution.

1.1 Digital Certificate Delivery

Digital certificates are delivered instantly after quiz results are finalized:

  • Delivery Time: Within 24-48 hours of result declaration
  • Delivery Method: Email to registered email address
  • Format: PDF download link
  • Validity: Can be downloaded and printed anytime

1.2 Physical Certificates (Optional)

For special events or upon request, we provide printed certificates:

  • Processing Time: 3-5 business days
  • Shipping Time: 5-7 business days within India
  • Cost: Free for national level winners, ₹99 for others
  • Quality: Premium A4 size, laminated certificates

2. Cash Prize Distribution

Cash prizes are distributed digitally. All prize money is transferred electronically to ensure secure and timely delivery.

2.1 Prize Distribution Process

  1. Verification: Winner verification within 24 hours of results
  2. Documentation: KYC documents collected (if required)
  3. Processing: Prize processing within 3-5 business days
  4. Transfer: Direct bank transfer or UPI payment
  5. Confirmation: Email confirmation sent upon transfer

2.2 Prize Distribution Timeline

Prize Amount Processing Time Delivery Method
Up to ₹1,000 1-2 business days UPI Instant Transfer
₹1,001 - ₹5,000 3-5 business days Bank Transfer/UPI
Above ₹5,000 5-7 business days Bank Transfer (NEFT/IMPS)

Note: Winners must provide valid bank account or UPI details. TDS deduction applies as per Indian tax laws for prizes above ₹10,000.

3. Merchandise Delivery

Limited edition OSIAN hoodies and merchandise are available for top performers and special event winners.

3.1 Available Merchandise

  • OSIAN Branded Hoodies (Premium Quality)
  • Event Participation T-Shirts
  • Winner's Trophy (for major competitions)
  • Limited Edition Badges & Stickers

3.2 Shipping Timeline

Item Processing Time Shipping Time Partner
Hoodies 5-7 business days 7-10 business days Delhivery/Blue Dart
T-Shirts 3-5 business days 5-8 business days Delhivery
Trophies 7-10 business days 7-10 business days Blue Dart

4. Shipping Within India

Shipping Partners

We partner with Delhivery and Blue Dart for reliable delivery across India. All shipments include tracking.

Shipping Costs

Free shipping for all competition winners. For additional merchandise purchases, shipping is calculated at checkout.

Tracking

All physical shipments include tracking numbers sent via SMS and email upon dispatch.

Delivery Areas

We deliver to all pin codes across India, including remote areas (additional time may apply).

4.1 Important Notes

  • No International Shipping: We only deliver within India
  • Address Verification: Ensure your address is correct during registration
  • Rural Areas: Delivery may take 2-3 extra days for remote locations
  • Unserviceable Areas: For extremely remote areas, we may use India Post

5. Delivery Issues & Solutions

Issue Solution Time Frame
Wrong Address Contact us immediately with correct details Within 24 hours
Failed Delivery Courier will attempt 2 more times 3 business days
Damaged Item Send photos, we'll replace immediately 7-10 business days
Not Received Track shipment, contact our support Within 48 hours

5.2 Return & Exchange Policy

  • Sizing Issues: Hoodie/T-shirt exchanges available within 7 days of receipt
  • Defective Items: Full replacement for manufacturing defects
  • Returns: Not accepted for personalized/customized items
  • Process: Contact support for return authorization
