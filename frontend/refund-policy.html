Refund Policy

At OSIAN, we strive to provide the best experience for our users. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be granted for paid services.

1. General Refund Policy

Most of OSIAN's services are free. However, for specific paid events, courses, or premium features, the following refund policy applies:

Important: Free quiz participation and basic platform access are non-refundable as they are provided at no cost.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds for paid services may be considered under the following circumstances:

Service Type Refund Eligibility Time Frame
Paid Quiz Events Full refund if cancelled 48+ hours before event start 48 hours prior
Premium Courses 50% refund if cancelled before course materials accessed 7 days from purchase
Subscription Plans Prorated refund for unused period 30 days from billing
Technical Issues Full refund if platform is inaccessible Report within 24 hours
Event Cancellation Automatic full refund Within 7 business days

3. Non-Refundable Items

The following are strictly non-refundable:

  • Digital certificates and badges already issued
  • Quiz attempts already completed
  • Services used or accessed
  • Processing fees for transactions
  • Donations to educational initiatives
  • Services where prohibited by partners/educational institutions

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, follow these steps:

1

Submit Request

Email osiancommunity01025@gmail.com with transaction details

2

Review Period

We review your request within 3-5 business days

3

Decision

You receive email notification of approval or denial

4

Processing

Approved refunds processed within 7-10 business days

5. Special Circumstances

5.1 Medical Emergencies

With valid medical documentation, we may offer:

  • Full refund for upcoming events
  • Credit for future participation
  • Deferred participation option

5.2 Technical Failures

If OSIAN experiences technical issues preventing participation:

  • Full refund if event cannot be rescheduled
  • Option to participate in rescheduled event
  • Credit for future events of equal value

5.3 Institutional Partnerships

Refunds for institution-sponsored events follow the institution's policies in coordination with OSIAN.

6. Refund Methods

Refunds are issued using the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 7-10 business days
  • Digital Wallets: 3-5 business days
  • Bank Transfers: 5-7 business days
  • Platform Credits: Instant (if chosen)

International transactions may take additional time due to banking processes.

7. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a refund decision:

  1. Submit a detailed appeal to osiancommunity01025@gmail.com
  2. Include all relevant documentation
  3. Our team will conduct a secondary review within 10 business days
  4. Final decisions are communicated in writing
